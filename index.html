#10 Zodiac (2007)

7.7/10

Director: David Fincher

Starring: Jake Gyllenhaal, Robert Downey Jr., Mark Ruffalo

Zodiac is a crime mystery thriller directed by David Fincher, starring Jake Gyllenhaal, Robert Downey Jr., and Mark Ruffalo. The film is based on the real-life hunt for the Zodiac Killer, who terrorized San Francisco in the late 1960s and early 1970s. Following journalists and detectives obsessed with solving the case, it explores themes of obsession, truth, and frustration. Known for its chilling realism and detailed investigation style, it has been called one of the best true-crime films ever made.
